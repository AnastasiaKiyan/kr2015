 Pincio hill to the church of Trinita dei Monti. This staircase was built by a little-known architect Francesco de Sanctis in the period
 from 1723 - 1725 years and is the official name of Scalinata di Trinit? dei Monti, which means literally "to the ladder
 Trinita dei Monti ", although it is popularly called simply, the Spanish Steps, and nothing to do with Spain, it has not. At the foot
 Spanish is the ladder square with a fountain in the form of a boat "Barcaccia"
