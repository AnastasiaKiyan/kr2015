5.Vittoriano
  This is a grand monument built in honor of the first king of a united Italy, Victor Emmanuel II. It is located on the
  Venice area on the slopes of Capitol Hill. This is perhaps one of the most amazing sights of Rome.
  Wide elegant staircase leads directly to the Altar of the Fatherland and the Unknown Soldier, the burial of the remains of the unknown soldier,
  who gave their lives for their country in the Great War of 1915 - 1918 years. At this place is constantly guard of honor. Above the chapel
  in a niche is a statue of Rome, left and right relief work Janelle and poobeim sides of the main staircase is a fountain.
