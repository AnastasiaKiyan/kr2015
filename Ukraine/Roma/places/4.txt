4.Kapitoly Capitol Hill - one of the seven hills on which the city of Rome originated. There is Kapitolsky temple, which is also called the Capitol, where the place of the meeting of the Senate and the National Assembly. Kapitolsky Hill is the smallest of the seven hills, is located near the southern bank of the river Tiber, and reaches 46 meters above sea level. On top of the hill stands the church of Santa Maria in Aracoeli, which can be reached by climbing the stairs with 122 degrees. At the bottom of the ladder are the ruins of a Roman insula. From the side entrance of the church can be reached directly at Kapitolskuyu area. Crowned ascent to the Capitol Square or to protect his two statues of Castor and Pollux.
