3. Museum of Art History and Museum of Natural History
Another great place that you must visit. Two huge museum located on either side of the square of Maria Theresa (Maria Theresien Platz) in the center of Vienna. You will be amazed magnificent architecture almost mirrored buildings with majestic towering over them octagonal dome. Collections founded in 1891, the Museum of Art History presents numerous works by Pieter Brueghel, Velasquez, Raphael, as well as other well-known paintings and numerous sculptures. Natural History Museum opened in 1889 - is one of the most important museums in the world. Here you can see an extensive collection of natural artifacts collected by the Habsburg dynasty.
