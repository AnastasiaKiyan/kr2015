5. Prater
If you want to have fun, while the most preferred place to visit would be a public park Prater. This is a huge entertainment complex, which offers a variety of leisure options for children and adults. The best option to enjoy a panoramic view of the city with a bird's eye view is an old (built in 1896-1897) Ferris wheel. The park can also be found karting, ice skating, roller coaster, a miniature railway, water slides, a hippodrome, a stadium, sports fields and a green recreation area. After a long and joyful day, you can have a tasty snack in the many restaurants and eateries located nearby. In general, the Prater is one of the best places for entertainment in Vienna.
