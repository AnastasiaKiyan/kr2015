2. Belvedere
Belvedere Palace is another landmark destination that is worth a visit during a trip to Vienna. The impressive palace complex includes two incredible residences - Upper Belvedere and Lower Belvedere. Two huge buildings are connected by a beautiful garden with blue ponds, fountains and statues. Belvedere Palace and the surrounding park is famous for its unique architectural style of the Baroque, which reflects the vision and tastes of Prince Eugene of Savoy. Currently, you can enjoy a variety of tours of the palace and the garden. Belvedere is an attractive destination for all lovers of art in its halls you can enjoy the works of Monet, Van Gogh, Gustav Klimt and other masterpieces.
