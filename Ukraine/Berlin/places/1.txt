1. Holocaust Memorial (Holocaust Mahnmal)
This is a series of blocks, a kind of park, built in honor of the memory of the Holocaust. Here you can walk between the blocks, and during the walk seemed to go deep down and then back again to rise. This unusual place symbolizes the Holocaust Stavan more severe during the war, and it was completed afterwards. You can make interesting photos. Undoubtedly, an interesting place to visit in Berlin.
